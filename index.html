Short Bio

I am a forth-year undergraduate student majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering, minoring in Computer Science and Data Science, at Shanghai Jiao Tong University Global College. I am currently a research assistant in Qu Lab, working on collective behaviors of soft active matter. I will work with Zijie Qu as a Master student majoring in Mechanical Engineering here.

I aim to understand non-equilibrium physical mechanisms of active matter systems and discover applications in engineering and medicine.
